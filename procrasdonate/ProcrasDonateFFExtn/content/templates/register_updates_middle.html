Updates, affirmations and thankyou notes

In order to help you keep track of your time management progress, our system will use your preferences to provide you with weekly feedback in the form of emails and/or alerts.

Due to user preference, your email address will not even be shared with the organizations you support. However, our system does allow your supported organizations to create automatic thank you notes and year-end updates so that they can let you know how your contributions help to further their missions.

By the way, although your ProcrasDonate records are anonymous, Amazon does include your full name (but not your contact information) on the transaction receipt that the organizations you donate to will receive.
