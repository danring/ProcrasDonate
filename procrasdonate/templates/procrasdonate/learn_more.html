Progress, Goals, and Budget

How often do you... watch video online? Read the news? Play games? Chat?

How much would you spend per hour to help you keep track of your time? What would you donate to your favorite charity to keep track of your time? ProcrasDonate's weekly scheduling goals help keep track of your progress and spending limits let you set a budget.

How It Works

Add our free software to your web browser and set your preferences.

Pledges will automatically be made on your behalf based on how many hours you spend online. Sites that you've marked as Time Well Spent will receive your donations directly. Time that you spend on sites you've marked as ProcrasDonation will instead benefit the charities that you've selected.

Turning Pledges into Donations

Pledges are linked to your Twitter username. Use Tipjoy to approve your pledges. We will send you occasional emails to remind you of any unpaid balance.

Why We Made ProcrasDonate

We started ProcrasDonate because traditional grant funding is unprepared to support innovative non-profits and quality online journalism. Our experience with individual donors is that they're ready to support innovation on their own terms. That's why ProcrasDonate is a flexible, user-centered funding source for charities and content providers.

We are the same people that brought you Buy It Like You Mean It. That is why bilumi is prelisted when you choose your favorite charities.
