Progress, Goals, and Budget

What is your time worth? What is information and entertainment worth?

How many pennies will you pledge per hour of ProcrasDonation? Weekly scheduling goals help keep track of your progress and spending limits let you set a budget.

How It Works

Add our free software to your web browser and set your preferences.

Pledges will automatically be made on your behalf based on your preferences.

Turning Pledges into Donations

Pledges are linked to your Twitter username. Use Tipjoy to approve your pledges. We will send you occasional emails to remind you of any unpaid balance.

Your Privacy is our Priority

The summary information that your browser shares with our community is anonymous. If you would like to be able to use the same account with multiple computers or web browsers then select secure account in your preferences (Coming soon!).

If you would rather keep all records on your own computer then select closed account so that no one, including us, can track your browsing history. We could not even access your records in response to a court order.

Select "Announce my Donations on Twitter" to enable automatic tweets of your donations and/or recipients.

Why We Made ProcrasDonate

We are the same people that brought you Buy It Like You Mean It. That is why bilumi is prelisted when you choose your favorite charities.

We started ProcrasDonate because traditional grant funding is unprepared to support innovative projects like bilumi and quality online journalism. ProcrasDonate is a flexible, user-centered funding source for charities and content providers.
