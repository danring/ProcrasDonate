How can ProcrasDonate help me?

Want to waste less time noodling around on the internet? ProcrasDonate's feel-good incentive and custom feedback can help. But that's not all.

ProcrasDonate is also a fun way to organize your charitable donations and build lasting relationships with your favorite non-profits.

Want to support quality online content? You can also set up a TimeWellSpent donation to pay the websites you care about for the time you spend using them.

What do I do?

First add our free software to your web browser.

Then go through the registration process: you'll use your existing Amazon.com account to set up whatever charitable incentive is right for you.

Sorting your websites into ProcrasDonation or TimeWellSpent is as easy as clicking on the question mark that will appear at the top of your web browser.

The ProcrasDonation meter next to the sorting icon is based on your weekly goals and limits. When the meter bars start turning red then you've procrastinated more than you want to for this week.

How do donations work?

Every hour you spend on websites you've marked as ProcrasDonation is a pledge to your selected charities. You can change your pledge amounts on your 'my settings' page.

Every hour you spend on websites you've marked as TimeWellSpent is a pledge to the website's content provider.

Whenever one of your pledges reaches a threshold of $10 then a donation is made directly and in full from your Amazon account to the intended recipient. Your voluntary transaction fees and voluntary monthly payments are treated as pledges and paid to ProcrasDonate.com at the $10 threshold.

Your 'my impact' page on our website, which can only be seen on your own computer, keeps track of your donations to date.

If you like, then our system will send you weekly emails on your time management progress and updates on our service.

Is my browsing confidential?

Yes! Privacy is important to us. Your can track your complete browsing data on your own computer. But we only store anonymous statistical data in order to measure our community impact.

Is it always recording?

No! ProcrasDonate only keeps track of the webpages that it thinks you are actually using. It stops recording your time when you switch to another application or browser tab. Most pages will also stop being recorded if you don't click a mouse or use the keyboard for 3 minutes. If there is flash on a page then the allowable idle time gets extended to 20 minutes (in case you're watching tv.) Finally, it won't record more than 10 hours of straight use even if you are spending all that time playing video games.

Will I get lots of junk mail?

We do not give out your email address. Not even to the organizations you are donating to. We will send you occasional thank you notes and year end updates from the charities you support unless you choose not to receive them.

How can I procrastinate less?

Three techniques have been shown to help people avoid procrastination: Goal Setting (breaking down projects into achievable steps), Stimulus Control (separating work environment from distractions), and Routines (establishing a comfortable schedule.) ProcrasDonate allows you to set your own Goals, track your online distractions (Stimulus Control), and create boundaries on your online Routine.

However, ProcrasDonate is not a medical treatment. Consult your therapist or doctor before incorporating it into a clinical routine.

Can I find out more?

We try to share as much information as we can find about procrastination on our blog. Check it out: http://procrasdonate.wordpress.com/

Can I make a suggestion?

Please! We want to adapt our system to meet your needs. So use our feedback link on the left hand of every page to tell us what you think. We'll send you a hand screened organic cotton work of art as a thank you!

How do I get updates?

You'll automatically receive add-on updates as we add new features. When Firefox asks if you want to install updates, click yes.

How do I stop using it?

To disable or uninstall this add-on use the FireFox menu "Tools -> Adds-on".

Disabling is like turning this add-on off. The data remains, but your surfing behavior is no longer tracked. You will also be unable to access your progress, impact or settings while this add-on is disabled.

Uninstalling this add-on may permanently delete your browsing and donating history. You can still view donations by logging into your Amazon payments account.
