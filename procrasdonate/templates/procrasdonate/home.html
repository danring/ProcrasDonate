Feel Good About Your Time Online   Start Now

ProcrasDonate helps you make hourly charitable donations for time spent on your selected web sites.

Our FireFox add-on provides convenient toolbar icons that you can click to classify the time you spend on particular websites.

ProcrasDonate: Help Out By Goofing Around

Use the ''hands offering a sprout'' icon to mark frivolous sites as ProcrasDonation.

Hours spent ProcrasDonating are micro-pledges to your favorite charities.

Time Well Spent: Support Quality Content

Hours spent on websites marked as Time Well Spent can also be micro-pledges that benefit those sites directly.

Use the "thumbs up" icon to mark worthwhile sites as Time Well Spent.

Set Your Own Goals: Feedback and Limits on Incentives

When your progress bar moves into the red you have passed your weekly ProcrasDonation and TimeWellSpent goal.

When all bars are full you have reached your limit — no more pledges will be made until next week.
