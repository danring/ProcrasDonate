Feel Good About Your Time Online

Give yourself a charitable incentive for good time management.

After registering... click the question mark on your browser to mark how you judge time spent on a particular website.

ProcrasDonate: Help Out By Goofing Around

Use the ''hands offering a sprout'' icon to mark frivolous sites as ProcrasDonation.

Hours you spend ProcrasDonating are micro-pledges to your favorite charities.

Time Well Spent: Support Quality Content

It's just as easy to donate to your favorite sites for the hours you spend using them.

Mark sites that provide you with worthwhile content as Time Well Spent using the ''thumbs up'' icon.

Set Your Own Goals: Feedback and Limits on Incentives

When your progress bar moves into the red you have passed your weekly ProcrasDonation goal.

When all bars are full you've reached your limit -> no more pledges will be made until next week.
