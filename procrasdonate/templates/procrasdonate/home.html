Feel Good About your Schedule

ProcrasDonate creates an easy, custom, and charitable incentive to help you feel good about being online.

Registration is free and takes about 5 minutes. After that just click the question mark on your browser to classify websites as either ProcrasDonation or Time Well Spent.

Time Well Spent: Support Quality Content

Pledge small donations automatically for good content as you use it.

Click until you see the "thumbs up" icon to mark sites as Time Well Spent that you consider worthwhile.

ProcrasDonate: Make Your Down Time Charitable

Pledge donations to benefit your favorite charities for every hour you spend goofing around online.

Click until you see the offered sprout icon to mark a website as ProcrasDonation.

Set Your Own Goals: Get Feedback on Incentives

The progress bar shows how close you are to your weekly ProcrasDonation goal. When it starts going red you've ProcrasDonated more than expected. When the bar if full you've met your limit and no more pledges will be made until next week.

Testers get a free shirt!

We need your feedback! ProcrasDonate and TimeWellSpent are being tested by users like you. Register and let us know what you think through our feedback page and we'll send you a hand screened organic cotton work of art... Thanks!

Expect Changes

After registering you'll be able to use new features as we add them just by updating your Firefox browser when it asks you to. Security is our top priority: so your personal data will be safe during our testing stage. Some of your settings may have to be reinstalled when we release our beta in June.
