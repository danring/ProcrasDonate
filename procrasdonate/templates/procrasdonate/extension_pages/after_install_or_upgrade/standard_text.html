1. Categorize websites

Click the icon that appears in Firefox's toolbar next to your browser's website address bar.

Click once to classify the currently viewed website as ProcrasDonation , twice for TimeWellSpent .

2. Monitor your weekly web usage

Click the meter to view your Progress in more detail.

Your meter reflects the goals that you've set for yourself and will reset every week.

3. Set up TimeWellSpent payments.

TimeWellSpent is our first advanced website classification feature. Use it as a place-holder or opt in to pay by the hour for websites that provide what you consider quality content.

4. Look for weekly messages

Each week you will receive messages at the top of your web browser. Messages will only appear while you're ProcrasDonating. Messages like:

  • Affirming updates on your procrastination progress.
  • Thankyou notes from your charities.
  • Occasional newsletters from your charities.
  • Updates on new ProcrasDonate features.

5. Vote for your favorite new features.

Our UserVoice page lets you make, and vote on, suggestions for what we should do next.

6. Tell your friends!
